📜 FMV Adjustment Rules (click to expand)
1. Base FMV: Highest price on 31-Jan-2018 (NSE vs BSE — higher one).
2. Split after 2018: FMV ÷ Split Ratio
3. Bonus after 2018: Original FMV unchanged. Bonus = NIL cost.
4. Rights after 2018: Original FMV unchanged. Rights = price paid.
5. Merger: FMV × (given ÷ received)
6. Demerger: Apportion per NCLT scheme.
7. Multiple actions: Apply chronologically.
