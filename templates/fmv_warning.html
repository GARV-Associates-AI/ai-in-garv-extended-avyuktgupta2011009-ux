FMV Data Required for Accurate Calculation

Some of your sold shares were purchased before 31-Jan-2018. Under Section 112A grandfathering, we need the Fair Market Value on that date to calculate tax correctly.

💡 What is Grandfathering?
For shares bought before 31-Jan-2018, the cost is the HIGHER of:
(a) Original purchase price, OR
(b) MIN of (FMV on 31-Jan-2018, Actual sale price)

Without FMV, the tool will use the original purchase price — which may result in higher tax than required.

💡 Where to find FMV?
• BSE: bseindia.com → Markets → Equity → Historical Data → 31-Jan-2018 (closing price)
• NSE: nseindia.com → Reports → Historical Data → 31-Jan-2018 (closing price)
• Use the HIGHER of BSE/NSE closing price as FMV
